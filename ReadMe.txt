1. Open the game project by Unity. Press play to start the game.

2. The intro is provided as Debug.Log when game start. 
   White start first, please click the leftmost paddle to roll. 
   If you made a mistake on select piece, click outside chessboard to cancel the selection.
   
3. Roll the paddle then select your piece to move, first click to select piece, second click to move. 
   Only click inside the chessboard unless you want to cancel your selection.

4. If your select piece is invalid, Debug.Log will print out "Can't land on your own piece." 
   Then player must try another piece until the move is valid.
   
5. Paddle with one white, four whites and four blacks will award a reroll. 

6. Landing on an opponent's piece is an attack, you exchange places.

7. Move each piece outside chessboard will gain one point, player who gains five points to win the game.

8. Please enjoy the game.
